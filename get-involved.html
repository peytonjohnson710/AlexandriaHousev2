Ways to Support Alexandria House

Volunteer

Individual volunteers help make our work possible through hands-on service, community support, mentoring, event assistance, donation sorting, and special projects.

To get started, please attend a volunteer orientation. Orientation is the first step for most volunteer opportunities and helps us introduce our mission, community guidelines, and current needs.

How to sign up:
Email us to ask about the next volunteer orientation date and available opportunities.

Email: info@alexandriahouse.org
Phone: (213) 381-2649

For Companies & Organizations

We welcome partnerships with companies, schools, faith communities, foundations, and nonprofit organizations who want to support Alexandria House through sponsorships, employee volunteer days, in-kind donations, workshops, or collaborative programs.

If you are interested in organizing a group service project, hosting a donation drive, sponsoring an event, or creating a longer-term partnership, please reach out directly.

Inquiries:
info@alexandriahouse.org

Phone: (213) 381-2649

Donate Needed Items

In-kind donations help provide families with daily essentials, household supplies, school items, hygiene products, and other critical resources.

You can support us by purchasing directly from our wishlist or by contacting us before dropping off donated goods so we can confirm our current needs.
