Alexandria House Program

Startup Sisterhood LA

Startup Sisterhood LA provides the Alexandria House community with the training, counseling, and access to resources needed to pursue, develop, and succeed in a career or small business.

Visit Instagram Explore the Program

Program Snapshot

13 weeks of guided instruction, support, and growth
1:1 mentorship tailored to each participant’s goals and next steps
Real training in business fundamentals, digital tools, and career-building skills
Why it matters

Building pathways to independence through entrepreneurship and career development.

Startup Sisterhood LA partners with successful entrepreneurs who share their knowledge, connections, and resources with participants. The goal is not just exposure, but real readiness: practical skills, stronger decision-making, and the support to pursue personal and professional goals with confidence.
