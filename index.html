Our Mission

Alexandria House provides safe and supportive housing for single women and women with children in the process of moving from emergency to economic stability and permanent housing since 1996. We are intentionally multicultural and anti-racist and are committed to addressing the systemic injustices that keep women and families disenfranchised and living in poverty.
