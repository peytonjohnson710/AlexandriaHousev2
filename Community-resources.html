Community Resources

Wellness services, basic needs assistance, and connection to community resources that support healing, growth, and long-term success for women and children.

01

Responding when families need immediate support

When a crisis hits, Alexandria House moves quickly. That can mean helping coordinate essential items, opening doors to urgent support, or making sure women and children are not left to navigate instability alone.

Our role is not only to provide resources, but to create calm, trusted connection in moments that can otherwise feel overwhelming.

02

Walking alongside our community with dignity and care

We support women and families through uncertainty with practical assistance, advocacy, and a deep commitment to belonging. From referrals and case support to safe spaces and everyday encouragement, our programs are built around real lives and real needs.

Every act of care is grounded in hospitality, dignity, and hope.

03

Meeting everyday needs that make stability possible

The day-to-day essentials matter. Meals, childcare, seasonal programming, transportation support, and access to household necessities can be the difference between surviving and being able to move forward with confidence.

We meet these needs with consistency and compassion so families have room to heal, grow, and plan for what comes next.
