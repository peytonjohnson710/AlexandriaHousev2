Top Chef

A volunteer-led, community-building food program for women and families in our Transitional Housing Program

Community Program

Cooking, Connection, and Care

Top Chef is a volunteer-led, community-building food program for the women and families in our Transitional Housing Program. We believe that enjoying a meal together creates lasting fellowship for both participants and volunteers.

Many of our participants have experienced ruptured familial relationships. Through the preparation and sharing of meals, we strive to foster a warm, communal environment that promotes healing, dignity, and belonging.

Top Chef provides families with a much-needed “night off” from cooking, allowing them to enjoy a stress-free evening with their children while being exposed to a wide variety of cultural food traditions.

Important Information

Program Details

When volunteering for Top Chef, please review the following guidelines to ensure a smooth and supportive experience for our families:

  • Provide enough food for 30–40 people when preparing or ordering a meal.
  • Please include a vegetarian option and consult our Volunteer and Resource Coordinator regarding other dietary restrictions.
  • If cooking on-site, arrive early so the meal is ready by 6:00 PM. Delivered meals should arrive by 5:30 PM.
  • Dinner begins at 6:00 PM, Monday–Thursday and starts with a community circle.
  • If you are unable to maintain your Top Chef date, please contact us immediately so we can arrange an alternate meal plan.
  • If running late, call (213) 381-2649 with your ETA.

Many Ways to Volunteer

Choose the option that works best for you, your group, and your schedule.

Cook On-Site at Alexandria House

Cook with your volunteer group in our Green House Kitchen. Volunteers provide all ingredients and prepare the entire dinner, then stay and enjoy the meal with our families.

Meal Drop-Off

Prepare a meal at home and deliver it to Alexandria House. Disposable dinnerware and soft drinks are always welcomed.

Support SUSLA Entrepreneurs

Order catering from SUSLA graduates who own small businesses. Email Jessica Flores-Garcia for our Vendor Directory.

Meal Delivery from Local Restaurants

Order from Koreatown restaurants and support local businesses. Please order enough food for 30–40 people.
