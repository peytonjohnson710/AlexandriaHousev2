Building Lasting Independence

Economic Empowerment

We equip women and families with the financial knowledge, career skills, and community connections they need to achieve lasting stability and independence.

Our Program Areas

Our holistic approach addresses every dimension of economic well-being, meeting each resident where she is and building toward where she wants to go.

Job Readiness & Employment

From resume writing and interview preparation to professional wardrobe support, we help residents enter or re-enter the workforce with confidence, connecting women with job placement partners and on-the-job training opportunities.

Education & Skills Training

We support residents pursuing GED completion, vocational certifications, community college enrollment, and workforce development programs — helping navigate financial aid, childcare, and scheduling along the way.

Financial Literacy

Workshops and one-on-one coaching cover budgeting, saving, credit building, banking basics, and debt management. We partner with community financial institutions to help residents build personalized financial plans.

Housing Stability Planning

Our staff assist residents in saving for deposits, understanding tenant rights, navigating rental assistance programs, and building the rental history needed to qualify for stable, affordable housing in Los Angeles.

Benefits Navigation

We help residents access CalWORKs, CalFresh, Medi-Cal, childcare subsidies, utility assistance, and other safety-net programs that free up resources and provide a bridge to long-term self-sufficiency.

Mentorship & Peer Support

Women connect with mentors who have navigated similar paths, participate in peer support groups, and build networks that extend beyond their time in our program — creating lasting relationships rooted in shared resilience.

Featured Program

Start Up Sisterhood LA

SUSLA provides the Alexandria House community with quality training, counseling, and access to resources to pursue, develop, and succeed in a career or small business — partnering with successful entrepreneurs who share their skills, connections, and knowledge.

Meet the Team

A 13-Week Journey to Self-Sufficiency

SUSLA's structured curriculum gives women the concrete tools to launch and grow a business or advance in their career. Through workshops led by community entrepreneurs and personalized mentorship, participants build real skills — and real confidence.

The ultimate goal is financial self-sufficiency and the achievement of each participant's personal goals, on her own terms.

The Path to Independence

Every resident's journey is unique, but our individualized approach follows a consistent framework designed to build confidence and capability at every stage.

Welcome & Assessment

A dedicated case manager meets with each resident to understand her history, strengths, and goals.

Personalized Plan

Together, resident and case manager create a step-by-step stability plan with clear, realistic milestones.

Skills & Resources

Residents engage in workshops, job training, financial coaching, and benefits enrollment.

Employment & Savings

With employment secured, residents begin building savings and preparing for permanent housing.

Permanent Housing

Residents move into stable, independent housing with ongoing support during their transition.
