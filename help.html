If you or someone you know is in need, we recommend the following resources:

For life-threatening emergencies

Call 911

For suicide and crisis support

Call 988

If you think you might be in an abusive relationship

Call The National Domestic Violence Hotline at 1-800-799-7233 24/7 for expert support for anyone experiencing an abusive relationship.
